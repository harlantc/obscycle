Frequently Asked Questions

In this section you will find responses to frequently asked questions about using the Peer Review GUI.

Where's the Search String?

Question:
I've entered a search string and the tool did not give a warning that the string was not found, but I can't find the string on the page. Where is it?
Answer:
Because of some finicky behavior with the table widget being used in the GUI, you may have to click on the bottom left scrollbar arrow a few times to make your proposal appear or on the right top scrollbar arrow to make you column appear or resize the table. As long as you did not get a warning, the string should be there.

Can't Edit Field that Panel Wants to Change

Question:
What do I do if the panel wants to edit a non-editable field?
Answer:
If there are fields that the panel would like to change, but the Edit Page does not allow it, you can put their request in the comments section or contact CDO if that is not sufficient for the panel chair.

Money in Time Field

Question:
Why does the archive and theory money amounts show up in the time columns?
Answer:
Money is treated like time in archive and theory proposals, so the amounts are displayed in the time columns. But, the running total columns for time do not include the archive/theory money.

New group not appearing in proposal edit page

Question:
Why is my new group not showing up in the proposal edit page?
Answer:
A new group will not appear in other parts of the GUI until a proposal has been assigned to the group. A related effect is that if all proposals in a group and moved to another group, the original group will disappear.

Triage column values

Question:
What do the different Triage values mean?
Answer:
ValueDefinition
YProposal was triaged with the Triage tool
NProposal has not been triaged
AProposal was triaged by panel in addition to the regular triaged proposals
RProposal was resurrected by the panel

Proposals not appearing in Resurrect page

Question:
Why are there proposals missing from the Resurrect page?
Answer:
Hide/Unhide triage values is a global setting which is picked up in all other views in the GUI, including the Resurrect tool. Go to the Tools menu and Unhide the triaged proposals.
